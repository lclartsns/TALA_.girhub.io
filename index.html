About TALA

Hi.

Say goodbye to the frustration of running out of stock or missing out on sales opportunities. With TALA, you'll have the data you need at your fingertips, so you can focus on what you do best - crafting high-quality handmade products.

Time-Series Application for Local Artisans

TALA helps local artisans who sell handmade products online to meet the demands of business and track their sales and inventory with ease. Through a time-series forecasting model and an easy-to-use dashboard, artisans can predict business demands and monitor their business performance real-time.

Application Features

What I can do.

Demand Forecasts

TALA's demand forecasting feature accurately predicts customer demand using an advanced time-series forecasting model. Say goodbye to the guesswork of inventory management and hello to being able to efficiently plan your stocks.

Real-Time Dashboard

TALA's dashboard provides a real-time view of your sales and inventory, so you can stay on top of your business anytime, anywhere. No more manual tracking or spreadsheets to manage.
